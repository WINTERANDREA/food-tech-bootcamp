## 3. What "enroll ten team members" actually means (decoded)

### The mechanism

**Anthropic Academy is public and self-serve.**

- URL: `https://anthropic.skilljar.com/`
- Platform: Skilljar (third-party LMS used for partner/customer education).
- **Anyone signs up with any email.** No invite code. No partner portal binding. No Anthropic account required. No corporate SSO.

The binding between "these ten people" and "FTB the partner" happens **manually, at the end**, when we click *"confirm completion here"* and submit the ten names/emails. Anthropic then validates against their certificate records.

This means we are **not blocked by an invite mechanism**. We can start the courses today, and so can any collaborator we point to the URL.
