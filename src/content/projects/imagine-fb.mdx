### The Question

Private label product development in food & beverage is slow. From concept to shelf takes months of back-and-forth between retailers, producers, and R&D teams. Most of this time is spent on information that already exists somewhere — competitor analysis, ingredient sourcing, regulatory compliance, pricing models.

### The Approach

An AI system that accelerates the product development cycle by synthesizing market intelligence, generating product concepts, and automating the documentation that slows down the process. Built for the intersection of artisanal capability and retail scale.

### Status

In development. First prototype being tested with a food retail partner.
