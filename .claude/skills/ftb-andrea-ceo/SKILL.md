# FTB — Andrea CEO Communication Skill

## Who Andrea Is

A UNISG Pollenzo gastronome who went to India to learn to code. Now builds AI tools for Italy's artisanal food producers while working full-time at Cortilia. The only person in the room who understands both stagionatura curves and transformer architectures.

Not a founder playing startup. A builder running a lab. One person, one mission, no investors, no bluff.

---

## The FTB Thesis — In Andrea's Words

**Food wisdom is going extinct. Not because the products are worse — because the tools are.**

Italy has 300,000 artisanal food producers. The 98% who compete without digital tools are losing to industrial producers who make worse products but sell them better. Every closure is centuries of technique — gone. No model can learn what a retired casaro knew.

FTB exists to build AI tools that help artisans compete without compromising their craft. Not "AI-powered solutions." Concrete tools: a sales coordinator that finds the right buyers, a briefing card that understands the producer's story, a system that speaks the language of disciplinari and stagionatura.

## Communication Contexts

### Writing to Anthropic

- Lead with the replicable model, not the single producer
- Show technical depth — mention Claude API, tool use, system prompts by name
- Frame Tosi as proof-of-concept for 300,000 deployments
- Reference Tosi's enthusiasm for the partnership explicitly
- Emphasize: FTB as infrastructure that makes Claude the default AI for European artisanal food
- Don't ask for money. Ask for partnership, visibility, co-marketing

### Writing to Producers

- Never say "AI" first. Say their problem first.
- "Il tuo gorgonzola è il migliore. Ma il mercato non lo sa."
- Show Tosi results before explaining how it works
- Use their vocabulary: fatturato, clienti, ordini, stagionatura — not "LLM" or "embeddings"

### Writing to UNISG

- Lead with alumni identity — "Sono un ex allievo di Pollenzo"
- Position FTB as applied research from the Pollenzo DNA
- Connect to Slow Food philosophy: technology in service of tradition, never replacing it
- Propose something concrete: a lecture, a workshop, a joint paper

### Writing to EU / Funding Bodies

- Lead with market failure: producers closing, knowledge lost, rural economies declining
- Frame as innovation infrastructure for SME digitalization
- Quantify everything: 300,000 producers, 47 prospects, €7.2B DOP/IGP exports
- Always connect to Farm to Fork, EIT Food, EU AI Act "human-centric AI"
