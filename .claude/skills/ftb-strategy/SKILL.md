## Legal Structure

- **Current:** Italian forfettario regime (P.IVA). Revenue ceiling €85K.
- **Trigger SRL Innovativa when:** revenue approaches ceiling, need to hold equity, specific EU bando requires it, institutional investment, or co-founder joins.
- **SRL Innovativa benefits:** no notary fees, flexible equity, 30-50% investor tax deductions, Invitalia eligibility.
- **Current advice:** Stay forfettario until there's a concrete reason to switch. Don't create overhead before revenue.

## Decision Framework

When deciding "should I do X?" — run through in order:

1. **Does Tosi need it?** → Do it. Tosi is the seed through June 2026.
2. **Does it produce multiple outputs?** → Prioritize. ≥2 flywheel outputs (case study, blog, pitch, EU material, template).
3. **Does it build a partnership?** → Anthropic, UNISG, or EU relationships are high priority even without immediate revenue.
4. **Can it wait until after June 2026?** → If not Tosi, not multi-output, not partnership-building — it waits.
5. **Does it BUILD or PLAN?** → If "plan," simplify into a build task or skip it.
